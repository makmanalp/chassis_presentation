  • Timestamp backwards, no ordering
  • Are strings
  • Wasted bits for UUID version, dashes
  • Are huge (128 bits)

Snowflake

10765432100123456789
  • Timestamp + Machine ID + Sequence Number
  • Unique
  • Time-ordered
  • No coordination
  • Not huge (64 bits)
